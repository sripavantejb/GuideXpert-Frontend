## 9. FAQ SECTION

### Section Header
**Got Questions?**

### Section Description
Find answers to common questions about becoming a GuideXpert Counselor.

### FAQ Items

**Q1: What are the eligibility criteria?**
A: To become a GuideXpert Counselor, you should have a passion for helping students, relevant educational background or experience in counseling, and the ability to commit to our training program. No prior certification is required.

**Q2: How long does the certification program take?**
A: The certification program consists of 25 hours of comprehensive training. You can complete it at your own pace, though most counselors finish within 4-6 weeks.

**Q3: What kind of support do I receive?**
A: As a GuideXpert Counselor, you receive ongoing support including access to our counselor toolkit, marketing assistance, performance dashboards, and a dedicated support team to help you succeed.

**Q4: Is there a fee for the program?**
A: The certification program includes all training materials, resources, and ongoing support. Details about any associated fees will be provided during the application process.

**Q5: Can I work part-time or full-time?**
A: Yes! GuideXpert offers flexible working hours. You can work part-time or full-time based on your schedule and availability. Many of our counselors work from home.

**Q6: What is the earning potential?**
A: Earning potential varies based on your level and the number of students you counsel. Beginner counselors can earn around ₹1,00,000, intermediate counselors around ₹5,00,000, and elite counselors can earn ₹10,00,000 or more annually.
